About

Few years ago I studied abroad thanks to the ERASMUS programs, which lead me to try leaving my life in a different Country every time a page turns. After I graduated from my French Masters degree in Computer Science, I had the opportunity to work in Canada.. I was first an intern with limited participation on projects, but I quickly progressed to a full-fledged developer and ended up leading projects. During this period I discovered the Game Engine Unity and I just love working with it. I had the chance to attend the 013 Unite in Vancouver and it was a formidable experience that made me feel like a member of a big family. Often during my free time I would experiment with new technologies first for my own knowledge but also to show the company what new technology was available and what could be done with it. As in all things, collective experiences culminate in a unique perspective. My perspective is rooted in the study of cities, architecture, traditional art, and language. Inspired by Walter Gropius and Bauhaus, I see design as a form which should exist unified with function. Web design is architecture in digital space and I strive to build designs to a purpose; craft efficient, valid code; and develop a strong online presence.
