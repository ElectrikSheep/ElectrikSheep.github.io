About

Few years ago I studied abroad thanks to the ERASMUS programs, which lead me to try leaving my life in a different Country every time a page turns. After I graduated from my French Masters degree in Computer Science, I had the opportunity to work in Canada.. I was first an intern with limited participation on projects, but I quickly progressed to a full-fledged developer and ended up leading projects. During this period I discovered the Game Engine Unity and I just love working with it. I had the chance to attend the 013 Unite in Vancouver and it was a formidable experience that made me feel like a member of a big family.

Often during my free time I would experiment with new technologies first for my own knowledge but also to show the company what new technology was available and what could be done with it.
