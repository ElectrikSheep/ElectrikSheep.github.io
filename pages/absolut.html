Absolut Greyhound World

A Unity3D project published on the iTunes Store and Google Play Store

A project to go alongside the Absolut Greyhound promotional Campaign at Dumdas Square in Toronto

Concept

The application display informations regarding the TV show and let the user 'merge' his face on the picture of a baby. Every week alongside the release of a new episode, the user would receive a notifcation and invite him to experience with a new Augmented Reality Experience.

Role

I wrote the code allowing the use to take a picture, then detect its face before cropping it and blend it on top of the picture of a baby.
Every Augmented Reality scenes were handled by me, they were designed using Unity3D and I had to build the transition from native iOS to a Unity3D scene.
