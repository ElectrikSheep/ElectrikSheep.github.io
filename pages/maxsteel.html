Max Steel A.P.P

A iOS project intergrating Unity3D scenes to display Augmented Reality Experiences

Concept

Using our special N-Tek Access Protocol Program (A.P.P), you can tour Max Steel’s top-secret headquarters in Copper Canyon and meet some of the N-Tek team. Just download the Max Steel A.P.P. for free to your iOS device.
The A.P.P. will introduce you to Max’s alien partner named Steel, his colleagues on the N-Tek team, some of his awesome technology and also some of his worst enemies. You’ll tour the N-Tek headquarters and use augmented reality to interact with 3D models of Max, his TURBO Cycle and the dreaded Earth Elementor. You’ll even have a chance to help Steel defend N-Tek when Elementor attacks by manning the TURBO Blaster.

Role

This application is clearly one of my favourite.
I was thrilled when the project lead chose me to be a part of the developer team. Thanks to the open space concept at the office I could see the animators working on the model from my desk, it had me jumping up and down for a week before being selected.
Even better, I was charged with the game side of the application. I won't talk about the code here, but it's an awesome feeling when your guinea pig (aka coworker) ask you to test the game one more time 'to be sure' or hear them swear when they die.
